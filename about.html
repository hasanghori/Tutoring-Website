About Us

Hi my name is Hasan and I am the founder of Connect Tutoring. As a tutor, I have seen a major problem in the industry. Teachers who have not taken your class in decades flood the market with unaffordable prices, causing cheap talented student tutors to never get discovered. My goal of Connect Tutoring, is to help students find tutors who are affordable, good, and have taken their classes recently.
